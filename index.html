chooks Journey

Here at Chooks we believe in good food, Good value and sustainability.

What makes us different..

We source free range chickens for all of our products and use biodegradable packaging to reduce our impact on the environment, No Plastic!
We strive to support and strengthen our community whenever possible, which is why much of our produce is locally sourced from right here in Artarmon.

  • Everyday we prepare fresh what we call a 'Dynamic Menu'.
  • Different dishes for you to choose from day to day.
  • The crowd favorites are often a fixture.
  • Large variety of vegetarian and vegan options.
